Mission

At BayBay Beach Resort, we prioritize family experiences, offering exclusive accommodations and amenities designed for all ages. Our spacious villas provide comfort and privacy, ensuring every family member feels at home. With kid-friendly activities and dining options, parents can relax knowing their children are entertained and well-catered for. From beachside picnics to supervised play areas, we offer ample opportunities for family bonding and adventure. At BayBay Beach Resort, create cherished memories that will be treasured for years to come.

Resort History

Established in 2014, BayBay Beach Resort stands as an exclusive haven for discerning travelers seeking a secluded retreat. Nestled along the rugged coastline, our resort offers unparalleled luxury in an idyllic setting, far from the crowds. Despite its rocky shores, BayBay Beach Resort boasts pristine waters and breathtaking views, inviting guests to unwind in utter tranquility. With a limited number of suites and personalized service, our resort ensures a truly intimate and exclusive experience for every guest. Experience the epitome of seclusion and sophistication at BayBay Beach Resort, where every moment is a celebration of exclusivity and serenity.
